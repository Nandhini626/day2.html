INDIA Vs AUSTRALIA

Australia stunned host India in Ahmedabad to win the men's Cricket World Cup for a record sixth time. Pat Cummins' men waltzed past the finish line with six wickets and seven overs to spare, quietening the wild support from the 92,453-strong crowd at the Narendra Modi Stadium.

On November 19, Australia defeated India by 6 wickets in the World Cup final and lifted the prestigious trophy for the sixth time. As Australia won the toss and elected to bowl first, the Indian team were put in a disadvantageous spot because, under floodlights, the pitch got better to bat and may have worked in Australia's favour. India's middle order, with key players such as Shreyas Iyer and Suryakumar Yadav couldn't contribute significantly.
